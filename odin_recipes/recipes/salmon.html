Baked Salmon

This mustard-crusted salmon is a wonderful way to prepare fresh salmon fillets in the oven. Be sure to make extra — your family will be begging for more!

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter, melted
  • 3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 ½ tablespoons honey
  • ¼ cup dry bread crumbs
  • ¼ cup finely chopped
    • pecans
  • 4 teaspoons chopped fresh
    • parsley
  • 4 (4 ounce) fillets salmon
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lemon, for garnish

Steps

  1. Step: Preheaat the oven 200 degrees C (400 degrees F).
  2. Step: Stir together butter, mustard, and honey in a small bowl. Set aside.

  3. Step: Mix together bread crumbs, pecans, and parsley in another bowl.

  4. Step: Brush each salmon fillet lightly with honey mustard mixture.

  5. Step: Sprinkle the tops of fillets with bread crumb mixture.

  6. Step: Bake salmon in the preheated oven until it flakes easily with a fork, 12 to 15 minutes. Season with salt and pepper, and garnish with a wedge of lemon.
