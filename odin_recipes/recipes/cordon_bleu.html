Chiken Cordon Bleu

Don't feel like going out tonight? You can easily make a gourmet restaurant-worthy meal in the comfort of your own kitchen with this top-rated chicken cordon bleu recipe. It's impressive, delicious, and way simpler than you think.

Ingredients

  • nonstick cooking spray
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 6 slices Swiss cheese
  • 4 slices cooked ham
  • ½ cup seasoned bread crumbs

  1. Step: Gather all ingredients.

  2. Step:Preheat oven to 175 degrees C (350 degrees F). Coat a 7x11-inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
  3. Step:Pound chicken breasts to 1/4 inch thickness.

  4. Step:Sprinkle each piece of chicken on both sides with salt and pepper. Place 1 Swiss cheese slice and 1 ham slice on top of each breast. Roll up each breast.

  5. Step: Secure with a toothpick. Place in the prepared baking dish and sprinkle chicken evenly with bread crumbs.

  6. Step:Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink, 30 to 35 minutes.

  7. Step:Remove from the oven, and place 1/2 Swiss cheese slice on top of each breast. Return to the oven until cheese has melted, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove toothpicks and serve immediately.
